The goal of blackjaqk is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand. The player can 'Hit' as many times as he wants, If you go over 21 you bust. If you choose to 'Stand' your turn is over, then it's the dealers turn. Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 16 or higher.
